About Voice of Environment

Organization for Clean Green and sustainable Environment Organization dedicated to “Environment, People and Mother Earth”

Voice of Environment is an established Non-Governmental Organization registered under Indian Society Registration Act XXI of 1860 on 22 September 2014 in the State of Assam having Headquarter in Guwahati, Assam India along with various states of India. VoE has been working since 2010 even long before its official inception in 2014.

 

Brief History:

While working during The ‘Maha Khumb Mela 2010’at Haridwar,Uttrakhand, India with a group of students studying Environmental Science on a wide range of campaign of public awareness & environmental conservation for devotees & tourists over tourism ethics & etiquette enlightening the role of river in human civilization we decided to set up a Youth Environmental Organization ‘Voice of Environment’ & this is how we incepted it full-fledged on 22 September 2014. We initiated working with the motto ‘Clean Green & Sustainable Environment’.

A Global Environmental broadcasting platform dedicated to all.
